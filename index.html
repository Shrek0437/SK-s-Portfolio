Automatic Faulty Street Light Detector

The Automatic Faulty Street Light Detector is a cutting-edge solution designed to streamline the monitoring and maintenance of street lighting infrastructure. Leveraging advanced technology, this innovative system employs sensors and data analysis algorithms to automatically detect faulty or malfunctioning street lights in real-time. With the Automatic Faulty Street Light Detector, municipalities and utility companies can significantly reduce response times to faulty street lights, minimizing downtime and improving overall street lighting reliability. The system continuously monitors the status of each street light, instantly flagging any anomalies such as flickering lights, complete outages, or irregular patterns. Upon detection of a faulty street light, the system generates automated alerts, notifying maintenance teams or relevant authorities for prompt intervention. This proactive approach not only enhances public safety by ensuring well-lit streets but also optimizes resource allocation and reduces operational costs associated with manual inspection and maintenance.
